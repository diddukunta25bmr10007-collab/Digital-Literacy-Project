Do’s
1.Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts
2.Share accurate and verified information
3.Maintain a professional and respectful tone
4.Think before posting anything online
5.Use social media for learning and networking
Don’ts
1.Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
2.Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
3.Don’t believe or spread fake news
4.Don’t use weak passwords or share your login details
5.Don’t spend excessive time on social media
