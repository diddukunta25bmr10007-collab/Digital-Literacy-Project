#  Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students)
##  Prevention Tips
1. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts (mix letters, numbers, symbols).
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media.
3. Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or bank details with anyone.
4. Always verify UPI payment requests before approving — receiving money does NOT require approval.
5. Avoid clicking on unknown links from emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages.
6. Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or App Store.
7. Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
8. Regularly update your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities.
9. Log out from shared/public computers after use.
10. Check website URLs carefully (look for HTTPS and correct spelling).
